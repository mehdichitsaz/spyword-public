SpyWord Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-18

SpyWord is a local pass-and-play word game for iOS. No account is required to play.

1) Data Categories We Handle

  • App Functionality Data (on-device): game settings and round state needed to run gameplay. This data remains on your device.
  • Support Communications (if you contact us): your email address and message content.
  • Advertising Data (Google AdMob): if ads are shown, AdMob may process limited device/app data such as advertising identifiers, IP-derived general location, and ad interaction metrics to deliver and measure ads, subject to your platform settings and consent choices.

2) How We Use Data

  • Provide core app functionality and keep the game working as intended.
  • Respond to support requests sent to us by email.
  • Support ad delivery, fraud prevention, and ad performance measurement through AdMob.

3) Third-Party Services

SpyWord uses Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob acts as a third-party service provider and may process data under Google's terms and privacy policies.

AdMob details: Google Privacy Policy

4) Sharing and Storage

We do not sell personal information. Gameplay data is not uploaded to our own servers for normal app operation.

When ads are active, relevant ad-related data is processed by AdMob. If you email support, we retain correspondence only as long as needed for support and legal compliance.

5) Your Choices

You can control personalized ads through iOS privacy settings (including tracking/advertising controls where available). You may also contact us to request access or deletion of information you sent directly by email, subject to applicable law.

6) Children's Privacy

SpyWord is a general-audience game and is not directed to children under the age defined by applicable law. If you believe a child provided personal information through support contact, email us and we will review and delete it when appropriate.

7) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

8) Contact

Privacy questions: spyword.app@gmail.com
